Who is Aske Falk?

Aske is a Malmö-based artist with roots in reggae. He has a bluesy voice and the songs give a positive feeling.The music mixes influences from rocksteady, soul and afrobeat with also a touch of country.

The sound is rooted in chewing rhythms where bass and drums build a steady groove. Funky electric piano and guitar drive the songs forward with a steady swing.

You got taken on a journey through the winding forest to sunny plains with lyrics that commute between melancholy and hope.
