Who is Aske Falk?

Aske is an artist based in Malmö who has a background in reggae music. His songs feature a bluesy voice and a positive energy. The music blends together elements of rocksteady, soul, afrobeat, and even a bit of country.

The music is characterized by rhythmic chewing sounds, with the bass and drums creating a solid groove. The songs are propelled by funky electric piano and guitar, which add a steady swing to the mix.

Get ready for a journey through a winding forest that leads to sunny plains. The lyrics of the music you'll hear on this journey will evoke both melancholy and hope.
